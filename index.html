Game Description

Evil Rush is not your typical tower defense. It’s cute, chaotic, and completely addictive.

Command a quirky army of evil creatures, defend your base against endless waves of invaders, and grow stronger with every tap. Whether you're strategizing hardcore or playing casually, this idle TD game delivers laughs, loot, and LOTS of explosions.
